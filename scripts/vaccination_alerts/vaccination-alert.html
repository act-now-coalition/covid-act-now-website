Colorado is now in Phase 1B, Tier 2 of vaccination

People who are eligible to be vaccinated now include:

Phase 1A

eligible
  • Highest-risk health care workers (Direct, prolonged COVID exposure)
  • Highest-risk individuals (Long-term care residents)

Phase 1B, Tier 1

eligible
  • Moderate risk health care workers (Less direct COVID exposure)
  • First responders
  • Coloradans age 70+

Phase 1B, Tier 2

most recently Eligible
  • Pre-K-12 educators and child care workers
  • Coloradans ages 65-69
Source: Colorado Health Department
See where and how to get vaccinated
on the Colorado Health Department website
Because eligibility varies throughout Colorado, you may also want to check your county or city’s health department website.
